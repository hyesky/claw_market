# :dart: Lead Gen — Prospect Research & Lead List Building

> Builds targeted lead lists, enriches contact data, scores prospects, and identifies buying triggers for sales teams.

## Overview
Lead Gen creates qualified prospect lists based on ICP criteria, enriches contacts with verified business emails and titles, and scores leads using fit and intent signals. It identifies buying triggers like funding rounds, hiring surges, and leadership changes to help sales teams prioritize outreach timing.

## Quick Start
```bash
mkdir -p ~/.openclaw/agents/lead-gen/agent
cp SOUL.md ~/.openclaw/agents/lead-gen/agent/
openclaw agents add lead-gen --workspace ~/.openclaw/agents/lead-gen
```

## Use Cases
| Request | Output |
|---------|--------|
| "Build a list of Series A SaaS companies" | Targeted list with firmographics, contacts, and email patterns |
| "Score and prioritize these 50 leads" | Ranked list with fit/intent scores and recommended sequence |
| "Find buying triggers for our target accounts" | Trigger events with signal strength and timing windows |
| "Estimate the TAM for our product" | Market segmentation with company counts and revenue potential |
